        #Our Cookie Policy:


        


    

    In the context of computers, cookies are small files that contain information about browsing activity.

    Whenever you visit a website, the website sends a cookie to the device you're using to access the website.
    Your device automatically stores the cookie in a file that's located within your web browser.

    When you revisit a site, the website will respond in a more personalized way,
    remembering your preferences, providing faster page load times and so forth.
